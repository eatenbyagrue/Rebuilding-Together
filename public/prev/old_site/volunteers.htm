What you can offer

One day of your time:  April 26th, 2008 or other days during the year.

 Skilled labor

We need carpenters, electricians, plumbers, roofers, painters, and contractors to address structural issues like leaking roofs, faulty wiring, and plumbing problems. We supply building materials and the support of other skilled volunteers. You provide your knowledge, experience, and a day of your time. Please bring your tools.

 Unskilled labor

Everyone can help. We need volunteers to help paint, clean, and perform numerous other small household jobs. We provide guidance, all material, and set up teams of 10-20 volunteers at each house. Encourage your friends, families, and co-workers to volunteer, too, and make a great day of it. Many hands make light work! Please wear your work clothes.  Children under 14 years old are not allowed to volunteer.

 What you get

  • The satisfaction of helping transform the home of a person who otherwise couldn't afford to make much needed repairs;

  • The camaraderie of the volunteers who you will work with at your house site;A good feeling you get from being part of 1,200 to 1,500 volunteers that made a big difference in Portland in one day

  • A t-shirt

  • Free food and drink.

 Volunteer Groups

Volunteer groups from companies, associations, churches, schools and other sources are a great fit with Rebuilding Together Portland projects.  Teambuilding, development, community involvement and great fun comes from working on a worthy project together.  We can put your group on a project or projects together to fit your goals on either the big day in April or at other times of the year.  We just need a contact person for a group to get started.

To sign-up, use our online form or the print and fax method if you want to volunteer.  (When you sign up, you will receive an email asking if you signed up with us.  We are an anti spam site.  Easy opt-out links will be included in emails we send to you.)  Please sign up at least two weeks before the big April project.

For more information about the program/application process, please call: 503-943-7515
